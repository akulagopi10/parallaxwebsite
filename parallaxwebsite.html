PARIS

Paris is the most visited city in the world. It has a population of 2.2 million, and is the capital of France. The Eiffel Tower is one of Paris's most iconic landmarks and was built in 1889 to celebrate the 100th anniversary of the French Revolution. It has become a global cultural icon of France and one of the most recognizable structures in the world.
